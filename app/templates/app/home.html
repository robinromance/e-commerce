Products

Milk products encompass a range of dairy-based foods derived from milk. Common examples include whole milk, which is rich in flavor and contains about 3.5% fat, and skim milk, which has a thinner consistency due to the removal of most of the fat. Flavored milk offers a sweeter alternative, often infused with popular flavors like chocolate or strawberry. Buttermilk, on the other hand, is a tangy, fermented milk product that serves as a versatile ingredient in various recipes. Dolda offers a wild range of milk products comprising of Fresh Milk, Butter, Ghee, Panner, Crud, Flavoured Milk, Doodh peda, Ice Cream and Skimmed Milk Powder.
